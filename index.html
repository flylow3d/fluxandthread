The Studio

Flux + Thread

Flux + Thread is a small stained glass studio run by Sarah. What began as a love of color and light has grown into a place to make — and now, to teach. The name nods to flux, the bit of chemistry that lets solder flow and glass come together.

Workshops stay small on purpose — just four seats — so everyone gets real hands-on time and leaves with a finished piece they made themselves. Handmade, with love.
