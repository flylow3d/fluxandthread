Meet the Maker · Flux + Thread

Hi, I'm Sarah

I'm the hands — and the heart — behind Flux + Thread, a small stained glass studio where I cut, foil, and solder one-of-a-kind pieces, and teach others to do the same.

I fell for stained glass [TBD — a line about how or when you got started], and what began as a love of color and the way light moves through glass has grown into a studio and a calling.

The name nods to flux, the bit of chemistry that lets solder flow and glass come together — and to the thread of craft and care that runs through every piece. Everything here is handmade, with love.

I keep my workshops small on purpose — just four seats — so everyone gets real hands-on time and leaves with a finished piece they made themselves.
